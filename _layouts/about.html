About us



Let's restructure the web together

We are a young startup that’s shaking up the world of data. From our homebase in London, we’re working hard to give people a totally new way to access data from the web. We have an amazing user base and were most recently the winners of the Beta PITCH competition at Web Summit in Dublin and one of the top 4 finalists of the Startup Battlefield at TechCrunch Disrupt Berlin. Backed by top European VCs and Valley-based Angel investors, our aim is to make a big impact in the world of data.
